# TigerGraph Savanna
### Contents
  * [Get to Know TigerGraph Savanna](https://docs.tigergraph.com/savanna/main/overview/#_get_to_know_tigergraph_savanna)


This documentation will guide you through the various features and functionalities of our brand new cloud-native Graph-as-a-Service (GaaS) management platform.
TigerGraph Savanna (<https://tgcloud.io>) offers a powerful and user-friendly environment for managing, analyzing, and exploring your graph data. It revolutionizes graph analytics by introducing a groundbreaking separation of compute and storage in its cloud-based graph database platform.
